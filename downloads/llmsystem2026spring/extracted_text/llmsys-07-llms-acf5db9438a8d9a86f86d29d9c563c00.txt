                                                                                      T5
          • Model Architecture
                 o Standard encoder-decoder Transformer
                 o Decoding: beam search
                        ▪ Beam width=4, length penalty=0.6

          • Model Size
                 o T5-base: 220 million parameters
                        ▪ 12 blocks, dff = 3072, dkv = 64, 12-headed attention, dmodel = 768
                 o T5-3B
                        ▪ 24 blocks, dmodel = 1024 , dkv = 128, dff = 16384, 32-headed attention
                 o T5-11B
                        ▪ 24 blocks, dmodel = 1024 , dkv = 128, dff = 65536, 128-headed attention

 Summary – Key Ideas in Modern Pre-
           trained LLMs
• Pretraining: Mask-labeled recovery of random spans + next
  token prediction

• Multitask supervised fine-tuning with instruction templates
  o T5, InsturctGPT

• Relative position: Rotary positional embedding

• Smooth activation: SwishGLU

• Sparse attention
