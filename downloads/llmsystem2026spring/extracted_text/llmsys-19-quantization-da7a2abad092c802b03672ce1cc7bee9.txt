       Direct Quantization Approach
• Using lower precision
  o converting parameters from FP32 to INT8 or INT4
  o perform all computation in lower prevision.

• Reduce model accuracy:
  o Loss of Precision ➔ accumulate quantization noise
  o Range mismatch ➔ values are clipped and lead to information
    loss
  o Quantization error ➔ rounding errors
